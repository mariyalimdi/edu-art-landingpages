About

Software Testing & Automation Training by Edu Art

 

Edu Art’s Software Testing and Automation training is designed to help learners build a strong foundation in quality assurance and modern testing practices. The program focuses on both manual and automation testing concepts, enabling participants to understand how real-world software applications are tested and delivered in Agile environments.

This training is ideal for freshers, career switchers, and professionals who want to enter or grow in the software testing domain. Learners gain hands-on experience with testing methodologies, test case design, defect management, and automation tools widely used in the industry. The course also aligns with industry standards and supports preparation for certifications such as ISTQB. 

By completing this program, participants enhance their job readiness with practical skills, real-time project exposure, and industry insights. Edu Art emphasizes activity-based learning, live demonstrations, and expert guidance to ensure learners are confident in both manual testing and automation, helping them secure better career opportunities in the software testing field.
